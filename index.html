About Us

The Ivy & Lantern – where taste meets passion! Since 2006, we've been creating unforgettable dining experiences with fresh, high-quality ingredients and a modern twist on classic British dishes.

Whether it's a romantic dinner, a family gathering, or a casual lunch with friends, our cozy interior, friendly staff, and live music make every visit special.

Opening Hours: Daily from 10:00 a.m. to 10:00 p.m.
